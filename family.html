My Big & Lovely Family

This is my big family. I practically live with my grandparents and some of my aunts all of my life, so I'm used to the big and noisy situation. We are close with each other, and what I can say about my family is that all of us are really loud and outgoing. Sometimes my friends will come to my house, and I can feel them being shy and awkward because my family is really straightforward. Fun fact about my family, all of us don't know how to smile while taking a picture, so sometimes the picture will turn out not nice and pretty.

My Mother

Mama, the love and light of my life. She is my everything, and without her, there is no me. There are no words that can describe her to me. She is my mother and father at the same time. She always tries to fulfill my needs and put her children first. Her love is constant, her patience endless, and her presence comforting. I admire her deeply, and I hope to grow into someone even half as incredible as she is. Mama used to work as a kindergarten teacher, but now she has stopped working because her knees are injured. Because of that, now she helps Abah with sending the children home from school. Mama loves to read, my sister and I got the reading habit from her. She has many novels in her wardrobe to the point that her wardrobe almost collapsed. Because of our love towards reading, we tried to go to PBAKL every year, and so far we have gone for 3 years straight and still counting.
